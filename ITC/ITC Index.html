Welcome to ITCAudit.COM

The goal of this page is to provide general information about our company, and allow for authorized customers to access the wealth of freight data we store.
Industrial Transportation Consultants (ITC), established in 1981, is a consulting firm for Traffic Management and Freight Bill Auditing. We take pride in our services that are oriented towards minimizing freight costs and expenses while keeping the best interests of our clients in the forefront. For more information feel free to browse our site.
I would like to thank all of our current users and customers for their feedback regarding our web-site. Keep it coming!
